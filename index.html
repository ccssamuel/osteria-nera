Our Story

A table in the dark heart of the city

Osteria Nera was born from a single conviction: that Italian cooking is first and foremost an act of memory. Chef Giulia Ferrante trained under her grandmother in Emilia-Romagna before refining her craft in Florence and Rome. Every plate we serve carries that lineage.

We source our flour from the same mill in Bologna our family has used for three generations. Our vegetables arrive each morning from the Mercato Centrale. The wine list is curated exclusively from small-production Italian estates, chosen on each annual visit.

  • 17 Years in service
  • 4 Michelin stars awarded
  • 100% Locally sourced produce
